Call for Papers

The Workshop on Medical Transformers (MEDXF), held in conjunction with ICIAP 2021, aims to attract contributions on the employment of Transformer architectures for medical imaging, with the objective of extending the state of the art this topic, presenting novel solutions to typical problems in medical image analysis and, just as importantly, investigating the limits and pitfalls of these new techniques in specific and socially-critical domains.

Expected submissions should cover, but are not limited to, the following topics:

  • CAD in medical imaging
  • Segmentation in medical imaging
  • Medical image reconstruction
  • Disease classification in medical imaging
  • Image analysis of anatomical structures and lesions
  • Multi-modal data analysis in medical imaging
  • Explainability and visualization of model features and decisions
  • Model training in data-limited scenarios
  • Assessing and reducing model bias
  • Self-supervised techniques for model pretraining
  • Medical domain adaptation techniques
  • Integration of imaging and clinical data
  • Self-attention mechanisms for 2D and 3D data
  • Transformer models for volumetric medical data
  • Unsupervised and semi-supervised learning with Transformers
  • From medical images to clinical diagnosis with Transformers

Accepted papers will be presented at oral and poster sessions, and the workshop proceedings will be published by Springer LNCS.
